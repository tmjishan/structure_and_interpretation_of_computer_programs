Mapping over trees Just as map is a powerful abstraction for dealing with sequences, map together with recursion is a powerful abstraction for dealing with trees. For instance, the scale-tree procedure, analogous to scale-list of Section 2.2.1, takes as arguments a numeric factor and a tree whose leaves are numbers.
