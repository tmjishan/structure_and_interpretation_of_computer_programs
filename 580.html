Exercise 4.30: Cy D. Fect, a reformed C programmer, is worried that some side eﬀects may never take place, be- cause the lazy evaluator doesn’t force the expressions in a sequence. Cy thus thinks that when evaluating sequences, we must force all expressions in the sequence except the ﬁnal one.
