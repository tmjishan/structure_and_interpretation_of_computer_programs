But there is an important diﬀerence between mathematical functions and computer procedures. Procedures must be eﬀective. As a case in point, consider the problem of computing square roots. We can deﬁne the square-root function as √ x = the y such that y ≥ 0 and y 2 = x . is describes a perfectly legitimate mathematical function.
