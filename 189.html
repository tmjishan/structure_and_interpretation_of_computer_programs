Additionally, when we uniformly represent structures as sequences, we have local- ized the data-structure dependencies in our programs to a small number of sequence operations. By changing these, we can experiment with al- ternative representations of sequences, while leaving the overall design of our programs intact. We will exploit this capability in Section 3.5, when we generalize the sequence-processing paradigm to admit inﬁ- nite sequences.
