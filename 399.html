It takes two input signals, A and B, and has two output sig- nals, S and C. S will become 1 whenever precisely one of A and B is 1, and C will become 1 whenever A and B are both 1. We can see from the ﬁgure that, because of the delays involved, the outputs may be gener- ated at diﬀerent times. Many of the diﬃculties in the design of digital circuits arise from this fact.
