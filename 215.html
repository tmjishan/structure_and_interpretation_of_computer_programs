Painter operations are based on the procedure transform-painter, which takes as arguments a painter and information on how to trans- form a frame and produces a new painter. e transformed painter, when called on a frame, transforms the frame and calls the original painter on the transformed frame. e arguments to transform-painter are points (represented as vectors) that specify the corners of the new frame: When mapped into the frame, the ﬁrst point speciﬁes the new frame’s origin and the other two specify the ends of its edge vectors. us, arguments within the unit square specify a frame contained within the original frame.
