To select the item at the front of the queue, we return the car of the pair indicated by the front pointer: (define (front-queue queue) (if (empty-queue? queue) (error "FRONT called with an empty queue" queue) (car (front-ptr queue)))) To insert an item in a queue, we follow the method whose result is in- dicated in Figure 3.20. We ﬁrst create a new pair whose car is the item to be inserted and whose cdr is the empty list. If the queue was initially empty, we set the front and rear pointers of the queue to this new pair. Otherwise, we modify the ﬁnal pair in the queue to point to the new pair, and also set the rear pointer to the new pair.
